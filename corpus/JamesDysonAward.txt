one national winner and four finalists are chosen from each country james dyson selects an international winner for the overall prize from 2007-2012 the international winning student or team received a £10000 prize £10000 for their university and a certificate from 2013-present the international winning student or team receives £30000 plus a variable amount to the university to help further develop the technology
